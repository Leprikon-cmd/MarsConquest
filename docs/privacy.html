Mars LogBook is a free companion application for the board game Terraforming Mars. It is designed to work entirely on your device.

Information We Collect

Mars LogBook does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers. It does not require account registration, user login, an email address, or a phone number.

Local Data

Game history, player names, settings, and statistics are stored only on your device. No game data is uploaded to the developer or any third party.

Analytics and Advertising

Mars LogBook does not include analytics services or advertising. The developer does not receive information about how the application is used.

Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

The application currently does not use third-party services that collect personal information. It does not sell, share, or transmit user data.

Future Online Features

Future versions may include optional online synchronization or community features. This Privacy Policy will be updated before such features become available.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to log.book.mars@gmail.com.
