Privacy Policy
Mars LogBook
Mars LogBook is a free companion application for the board game Terraforming Mars.
Your privacy is important. This application is designed to work entirely on your device.
Information We Collect
Mars LogBook does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.
The application does not require:
account registration;
user login;
email address;
phone number.
Local Data
The application stores game history, player names, settings, and statistics only on your device.
No game data is uploaded to the developer or any third party.
Analytics
Mars LogBook does not include analytics services.
The developer does not receive information about how the application is used.
Advertising
Mars LogBook contains no advertising.
Third-Party Services
The application currently does not use third-party services that collect personal information.
Data Sharing
The application does not sell, share, or transmit user data.
Future Online Features
Future versions may include optional online synchronization or community features.
If those features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated before they become available.
